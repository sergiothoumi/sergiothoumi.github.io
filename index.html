About Me

Check out the Graph to LaTeX tool where you can draw a graph and get its corresponding TikZ code. This was initially inspired by the app of Dr. Edouard Bonnet which you can check out here.

I am a pre-doctoral research assistant who is broadly interested in algorithm design and computational complexity. My current and past work spans graph algorithms, classical complexity theory (i.e., NP-hardness), and parameterized complexity. I am actively seeking a research internship where I can either deepen my expertise in these areas or explore new directions. In particular, I am looking to get into fast graph algorithms, dynamic algorithms, and approximation algorithms. I plan to apply to PhD programs for the Fall 2026 cycle.
